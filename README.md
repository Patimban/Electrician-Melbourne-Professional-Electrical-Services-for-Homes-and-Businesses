**Frequently Asked Questions**
**How do I know if I need an electrician?**

If you notice flickering lights, circuit breakers tripping frequently, burning smells, or faulty power outlets, it's best to contact a licensed electrician for an inspection.

**Can electricians install EV chargers?**

Yes. Many Melbourne electricians specialize in installing electric vehicle charging stations for residential and commercial properties.

**How often should electrical systems be inspected?**

Experts recommend periodic inspections, especially for older homes, rental properties, and commercial buildings, to ensure ongoing safety and compliance.

**Are emergency electricians available 24/7?**

Many electrical companies offer emergency services outside normal business hours to handle urgent issues such as power failures and dangerous electrical faults.

**Why should I upgrade my switchboard?**

Older switchboards may not support modern electrical loads. Upgrading improves safety, reliability, and compliance with current electrical standards.
